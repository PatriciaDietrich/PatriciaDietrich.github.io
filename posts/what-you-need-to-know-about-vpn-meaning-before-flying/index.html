1. What is VPN and how it works?#

A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a network of secure servers that provides a safe and encrypted connection from an external location to your internal resources. It is important to understand how a VPN works so you can know what to expect when using it. A VPN works by routing your traffic through an encrypted tunnel between the remote location and the destination. This tunnel keeps your data secure and prevents your internet service provider (ISP) from seeing what websites and applications you are using. Inside the tunnel, your data is encrypted so that if it were intercepted, it would be unreadable. The VPN server also authenticates the connection between your device and the destination, ensuring that any data you transmit is sent to the correct server. Finally, the VPN server also acts as a gateway between your device and the internet, allowing you to browse the web anonymously and securely. With a VPN, you can keep your online activity private and access remote networks securely. Understanding how a VPN works can help you get the most out of it.
