Q. What is a VPN?

A. A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a technology used to create a secure connection between two or more networks, allowing users to access information and resources on a private network from a public or shared network. With a VPN, users can access websites and services that are otherwise inaccessible due to their geographical location or network restrictions.
