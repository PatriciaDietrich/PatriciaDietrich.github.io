Setting up a VPN on an iPhone is easy and only takes a few minutes.

First, open the Settings app on your iPhone and scroll down to the General section. Tap VPN to open the VPN menu. From there, select “Add VPN Configuration” to begin the set up process.

You’ll then be prompted to enter the server, account, and password details for the VPN you’re setting up. Once you’ve entered the information, tap “Done” at the top right of the screen.

To connect to the VPN, go back to the VPN settings screen and slide the Connect On Demand switch to On. This will ensure that your iPhone always connects to the VPN whenever an application tries to access the Internet. Finally, toggle the VPN switch to On to activate the VPN and protect your data.

Now you’re ready to browse the Internet securely and access websites that may be blocked in your area. Make sure to remember the login credentials you used to set up the VPN so you can reconnect quickly when needed.
