Q. What is a VPN? A. A Virtual Private Network (VPN) is a service that provides a secure connection between a computer or mobile device and a remote server. It allows users to access websites and other online resources from any location with an internet connection. This is accomplished by routing all traffic through an encrypted tunnel, providing a high level of privacy and security for online activities.

Q. What is the benefit of using a VPN? A. Using a VPN helps to protect your online activity from prying eyes by encrypting it and routing it through a secure server. This way, your activity cannot be monitored, tracked, or otherwise accessed by third parties.
